Grandma secret Apple pie

The apple pie recipe hs been in my genrtion for years

Ingredients

  • 1\2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp floor
  • 6 medium size apples

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven for 30 seconds
  2. In bowl mix the floor and sugar
  3. toss the apples into the mixture
